It all changed when the fierce Lama Del Rey came to this land and enslaved all the donkeys.

You also need to provide food for your son, Piñatino. The kid has not been the same since his mum, Asseñora, left for a a career in Cinema.
There have been rumors of a future uprising but you don't really believe... Maybe the guys at the Spart-Ass-Cuss can tell you more.
Oh, and by the way, here at Donkey Island, carrot is the only food and shell's the only currency.
